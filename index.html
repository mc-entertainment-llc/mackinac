Your Island Guide
An exciting history. Countless myths. Unique stories.
Explore the secrets of Mackinac Island at your own pace for a truly memorable vacation.
Fall into the rhythm of an island that appears to be frozen in time, where horse-drawn carriages, classical architecture and traditional delicious fudge are still part of the present.
The Mackinac Island App offers you access to six engaging tours that cover all the important stories, sights and landmarks in over four hours of unique audio content.
The app also includes a comprehensive Information Guide with all the businesses, restaurants and points of interest worth visiting, so you can be sure you’ll have a trip to remember.
Download the app and get instant access to the FREE Ferry Tour that will give you a taste of Mackinac Island’s uniqueness.

What does Mackinac Island App offer?
Discovering Mackinac Island is a wonderful adventure, but with our engaging and informative app, you can elevate your experience by discovering all the myths, stories and details that make this island truly unique.
Audio Tours

Ferry Tour: FREE sample to keep you entertained until you arrive at the island

Grand Mackinac Island Tour: covers the most fascinating places and details about Mackinac Island

West Island Tour: digs deeper into the western side of the island’s landmarks and their stories

East Island Tour: unravels the best sights and sites on the eastern side of the island

Historic Mackinac Island Tour: delves into the stories and legends of Mackinac Island’s captivating history

Turtle Tour: specially created for kids but can be enjoyed by the entire family

Over 70 audio tracks and more than four hours of unique content.
